Why Bamboo

Nature's most efficient renewable resource, engineered by evolution for strength and sustainability.

Fastest-growing renewable plant

Bamboo shoots can grow up to 91cm in a single day. That's faster than any tree species on Earth.

Absorbs more carbon than trees

Bamboo absorbs up to 35% more CO₂ than equivalent stands of trees and releases more oxygen.

Regenerates without replanting

Once harvested, bamboo regrows from its root system without replanting.

Naturally antibacterial

Bamboo contains natural antimicrobial properties that resist bacteria and fungi growth.

~90% less land per sheet

Bamboo yields approximately 10 times more pulp per hectare than tree plantations, dramatically reducing the land footprint of every tissue you use.

Impact Comparison 20x

Bamboo grows 20 times faster than hardwood trees used in conventional tissue production.

A Lower-Impact Alternative to Wood Pulp

Bamboo regenerates quickly and does not require tree cutting.
